About Me

Welcome

I'm David Reyes, a Mechanical and Aerospace Engineer currently pursuing my M.S. in Mechanical Engineering at Columbia University, specializing in Robotics and Control Systems. I hold a dual B.S. degree in Mechanical and Aerospace Engineering from the University of Florida, where I also minored in Mathematics.

My passion lies at the intersection of precision mechanics and intelligent autonomy. From designing control algorithms for autonomous underwater vehicles to mentoring undergraduates in finite element analysis, I thrive in environments that blend analytical rigor with hands-on prototyping.

Outside the classroom, I’m actively exploring PCB design and machine learning applications in robotics. My goal is to build robust, adaptive systems that improve human-robot collaboration across industries.
